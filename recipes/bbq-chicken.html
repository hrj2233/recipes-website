BBQ Chicken

Description

Nice crispy chicken, with tangy taste. It is best if bone-in chicken pieces used.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ cups cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 2 pounds cut up chicken pieces

Steps

  1. Heat grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small skillet combine the oil, vinegar, salt and pepper and put over low heat.
  3. Add the poultry seasoning while stirring constantly; when sauce mixes well and starts to bubble, it is done.
  4. Place chicken on hot grill and brush with sauce.
  5. Grill for 45 to 60 minutes, turning every 5 to 10 minutes, and brush chicken with sauce after each turning.
  6. Grill until chicken is done and juices run clear. (Note: Be sure to keep an eye on the chicken as it cooks, as it tends to have flair ups due to the oil and chicken drippings!)
